Magic Etch A Sketch Screen

etch-a-sketch.js

This little project is inspired by the classic toy from the 1950s.

The Etch A Sketch toy was invented in the late 1950s by André Cassagnes, an electrician with Lincrusta Co, who named the toy L'Écran Magique (The Magic Screen). In 1959, he took his drawing toy to the International Toy Fair in Nuremberg, Germany.
